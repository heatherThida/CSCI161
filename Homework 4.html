  1. (100 pts.) Write a C++ program to solve word search puzzles using an unordered_set<std::string>.

    A word search puzzle is an N×MN×M array of lower-case letters, and the objective is to locate and output all dictionary words of length 6 or more that appear in this grid horizontally, vertically, or diagonally and either forwards or backwards (but no wrap-arounds).

    For example, given the following puzzle, 

    r d z i t p m f 
t e k a n s t d 
t i b b a r o o 
k e l a h w a a 
c j i e p n d k 
s d e o e m z i 
h z i y l a t x 
i s h h e e l s
    the output should be: 
    elephant from (7, 4) to (0, 4)
rabbit from (2, 5) to (2, 0)

    Your code must

    1. be able to handle puzzles of arbitrary dimensions;
    2. use the dictionary words.txt;
    3. use an unordered_set<std::string> to store the dictionary;
    4. sort the output list of words of length 6 or more, along with their starting and ending positions alphabetically.
